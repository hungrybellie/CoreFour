How does Core Four work?



You choose four categories, and an item from each that you really want. If these items stay in your core four for over a month, you should consider buying them. If not, you can replace an item at anytime, also restarting your timer for that item.









How does this help?



Core fours help people beat the bad habit of overconsumption or impulse buying, which have negative effects on our environments. Why buy more than you really need, if it will only end up in a landfill or polluting our seas in a few months time?


Core four can also be implemented as a saving strategy for those looking to save money.









What's the cost?



There is no cost! Core Four is simply here to help you and our planet.
